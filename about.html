About me

My Values

UX Design is always changing, so I a dedicated to always staying up to date with the latest trends. To be willing to learn is an absolutely necessary trait.

Empathizing

Since the beginning of my professional career in massage therapy, it was important to be able to put myself in my patient's shoes. Now I use the same skill I have used in the medical field into UX Design.

Passion

User Experience is such a dynamic field that requires curiosity and a willingness to connect with people. I am always excited to meet with folks and not only come up with solutions, but also learn something new.

Stay Creative

When I am not doing UX Design, I dedicate my time to artistic hobbies. Not only does this keep my creative juices flowing, but it also reduces burnout and helps keep me engage in coming up with new ideas.
